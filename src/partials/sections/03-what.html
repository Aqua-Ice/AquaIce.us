What is it?

It's non-dairy, gluten-free, and peanut-free, yet it's still creamy and delicious. Unlike Shaved Ice or a Snow Cone, which are just syrup poured over crushed ice, Aqua Ice is carefully blended into a smooth, creamy consistency, much like Ice Cream or Sorbet. It's the best parts of Ice Cream and Snow Cones mashed into one.

With 18 delicious flavors, your whole family is sure to love this refreshing treat. We even have a sugar-free option!
